Mallika Sharma

PhD Candidate, Department of Economics
University of Calgary

I am an environmental economist specializing in energy and transportation economics, with a focus on electric vehicle usage behaviors, charging infrastructure policy, and demand-side management with EV charging. My research combines advanced econometric methods with policy analysis to understand electricity markets and the integration of clean technologies.

I bring expertise in both qualitative and quantitative methods, including econometric modeling and causal inference. My work contributes to evidence-based climate and energy policy, addressing challenges in clean transportation system and policy.
